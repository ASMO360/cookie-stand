Pat's World Famous Seattle Salmon Cookies


On a grey Seattle day our founder Pat had developed a recipe for a coffee-time confection of two signature Seattle icons and created the world famous Salmon Cookies.

They're cookies made into the shape of a salmon with the finest ingredients that our famous local Salmon. Salmon cookies are an a suprisingly tasty treat and an ideal match for a steaming cup of coffee. Tourists and locals have fallen in love with these world famous salmon cookies.

Here at Pat's World Famous Salmon Cookies we not only have a passion for Salmon Cookies but, also the conservation of Salmon habitats. Not only does a portion of our Salmon Cookie sales go towards the Raging River Conservation Group, but 100% of our t-shirt sales goes towards the fund.
